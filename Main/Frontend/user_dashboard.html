Protein Claim Calculation
Based on the PDCAAS (Protein Digestibility Corrected Amino Acid Score) and protein percentage, we calculate whether a protein source meets regulatory requirements. The formula below allows us to evaluate whether the protein qualifies for a claim based on set thresholds.
Protein Claim Formula:
RACC = (Protein Percentage × PDCAAS) ÷ 100
Protein Claim Categories:
If RACC < 5 → No Claim
5 ≤ RACC < 10 → Good Source
RACC ≥ 10 → Excellent Source
The RACC value determines the protein source's eligibility for claims. A higher RACC indicates a better protein quality and makes the source eligible for "Good" or "Excellent" source labels.
